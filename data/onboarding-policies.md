# Vincent Bank — Onboarding Policies (Demo)

> This is synthetic policy content for demonstration purposes only.

## Personal Details

- **Name:** Enter your legal name as it appears on your government-issued ID.
- **Date of birth:** Use format YYYY-MM-DD (e.g. 1990-01-25). You must be at least 18 years old to open an account.
- **Email:** We use this for account notifications and One-Time Pins. Use an address you actively monitor.

## Address

- **Proof of address:** Accepted documents include a utility bill, bank statement, or government letter dated within the last 90 days showing your full name and address.
- **PO Box:** We cannot accept PO Box addresses as the primary address; a residential address is required.
- **Zip code:** Must be a valid 5-digit US zip code (or 9-digit ZIP+4 format).
- **Supported countries:** United States only in this demo.

## ID Document

- **Accepted IDs:** US Passport, US Driver's License, or US State ID.
- **Expired IDs:** We do not accept expired identification documents.
- **Image upload:** Upload a clear, well-lit photo of the front of your ID. Blurry or obscured images may not extract correctly — you can always edit the pre-filled fields manually.
- **Data accuracy:** All extracted fields are editable. You are responsible for confirming the extracted data is correct before submitting.

## Review & Submit

- **Editing:** You may go back to any step to correct information before submitting.
- **Submission:** By submitting, you confirm all information is accurate. This demo does not transmit any data externally.
- **Processing:** In a real application, identity verification takes 1–3 business days.

## Privacy

- This is a prototype using synthetic data only. No real personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted.
- In production, Vincent Bank would comply with applicable data protection regulations.
